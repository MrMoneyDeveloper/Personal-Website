Short Bio

Systems thinking with implementation discipline

Farhaan works across Zendesk implementations, automation, AI-assisted workflows, data operations, and technical delivery. His focus is on designing support environments that are structured enough to scale without becoming chaotic.

The work regularly spans forms, routing logic, help centre structure, messaging flows, analytics-minded data design, and connective tissue between support operations and internal tooling.

Extended Bio

From problem framing to operational refinement

His background includes scripting, systems work, routing, storage, dashboarding, software delivery, and team leadership. Across projects, he moves from clear problem framing to working scaffold creation, testing, documentation, and iterative refinement based on evidence.
